WHY TEDx?

TED, which stands for Technology, Entertainment, and Design, is a platform for infinite ideas. As an international nonprofit organization, TED thrives on creativity, embracing diverse perspectives, and giving a voice to every idea—big or small. TEDx provides a stage for speakers of all styles, backgrounds, and languages, encouraging them to share insights ranging from groundbreaking business innovations to deeply personal life experiences.

WE THE BVRITH...

In a world woven with countless perspectives, TEDxBVRITH stands as a platform where imagination meets expression. Our speakers break open the caps of their minds, unleashing thoughts that challenge norms, spark inspiration, and encourage fearless exploration. At TEDxBVRITH, we believe in fostering that courage—the courage to challenge the unknown, to inspire others, and to create an impact that lasts beyond the stage.
